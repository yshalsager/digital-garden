-   `Styles -> Manage Styles -> Icon: Page Styles` (4th icon below **Styles**)  
-   `Right click` on _`Default Style`_ `-> Modify -> Tab: Footnote` ( or right click on the page style you are using)  
-   Select position in drop down _Position_.
